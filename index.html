Common questions

Do I need an account?
Yes. Atlas keeps your tasks, notes, and journal in sync across your devices, so it uses a simple email and password account.

Is my data private?
Yes. No ads, no tracking, and we never sell your data. You can also lock your journal behind Face ID. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I delete my account?
Open Atlas, go to Settings, and choose to delete your account. This removes your account and the data stored with it.
